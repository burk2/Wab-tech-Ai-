Our Programs

1. Prompt Engineering for Everyone (No-Code AI Training)

This beginner-friendly training empowers people of all ages and backgrounds to effectively interact with AI tools like ChatGPT, Microsoft Copilot, Canva AI, and more — without any coding required.

Who is it for?

Students, teachers, entrepreneurs, farmers, professionals, and anyone curious about AI who lacks programming skills.

What you’ll learn:

  • Writing clear and powerful AI prompts to get the best results
  • Using AI for writing, research, marketing, customer support, and everyday tasks
  • Applying AI tools in agriculture, business, education, and climate action
  • Basics of responsible AI use and prompt engineering

2. Artificial Intelligence, Data Science & Machine Learning Courses

A comprehensive track designed to teach beginners and intermediate learners practical skills in AI, Data Science, and Machine Learning.

Who is it for?

Beginners with little or no coding background, intermediate learners aiming to deepen their AI knowledge, students, professionals, and tech enthusiasts.

What you’ll learn:

For Beginners:

  • Introduction to AI and its applications
  • Data collection, cleaning, and visualization basics
  • Fundamentals of machine learning (supervised and unsupervised)
  • Simple tools including Excel, no-code platforms, and basic Python
  • Responsible AI practices

For Intermediate Learners:

  • Python for AI (NumPy, Pandas, Scikit-learn)
  • Building ML models: classification, regression, clustering
  • Introduction to deep learning and neural networks
  • Hands-on projects like sales forecasting and crop prediction

Who is it for?

Businesses, NGOs, community groups, creators, farmers, health workers, climate activists — anyone looking to apply AI practically.

What you’ll get:

  • Live demonstrations of AI tools tailored to specific sectors
  • Case studies and best practices for business automation, digital content creation, climate monitoring, healthcare, and agriculture
  • Interactive Q&A and problem-solving
  • Guidance on ethical AI use
